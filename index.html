About Carbon-Meter

Carbon-Meter is a project dedicated to tracking carbon emissions and green energy usage worldwide. Our mission is to raise awareness about climate change and support global efforts to reduce carbon footprints. By providing accurate and up-to-date data, we empower individuals, businesses, and policymakers to make informed decisions for a sustainable future.

Carbon-Meter combines scientific research, open data, and modern visualization tools to make climate information accessible and easy to understand. We aim to highlight not only the challenges but also the progress made in adopting renewable energy, reducing pollution, and transitioning to greener economies.

Through interactive charts, global comparisons, and detailed country-level insights, users can explore how carbon emissions evolve over time and how sustainable energy solutions impact the future of our planet. Whether you are a student, a company, or a policymaker, Carbon-Meter is here to provide transparency and actionable knowledge.

Together, we believe that raising awareness, sharing responsibility, and supporting innovative solutions are the keys to building a healthier environment and a more sustainable future for everyone.
